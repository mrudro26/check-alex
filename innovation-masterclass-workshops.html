INNOVATION MASTERCLASS WORKSHOP TOPICS?

In order for an innovation ecosystem to flourish and for you to achieve your highest level of success as an innovative leader or company, an innovative mindset must be present in all aspects of your organizational strategy.  

But an innovative mindset is not nearly enough.  Alexander Blass teaches you specifically how to innovate.  He developed a proprietary business innovation framework which he named the Wheel of Innovation™.  This framework has three prongs, or spokes of the wheel.  Based on this proprietary framework, Alexander conducts the following 3 highly popular masterclass training workshops worldwide on critical areas of business innovation:

EXPLORE ALEXANDER’S MASTERCLASS TRAINING WORKSHOPS

MASTERCLASS 1 - INNOVATION IN LEADERSHIP, MINDSET, PEOPLE, AND CULTURE

THE HUMAN SIDE OF INNOVATION: HOW TO AWAKEN YOUR INNOVATOR WITHIN

Description: Leadership through innovation and creativity are no longer optional, but essential. Learn how you can reinvent yourself as an innovative leader as well as your company to get on the path to innovation today, think about “what’s next” and challenge the status quo. In this captivating and high-energy interactive session audiences will:

  • Explore 5 essential principles and characteristics of leading innovators and companies
  • Learn how you can infuse an innovative mindset and culture into your organization and your life, regardless of role or title
  • Understand the differences of idea-based, challenge-based, and open innovation approaches and how to motivate, encourage and reward the public to innovate for you by learning about gamification and other methods
  • Discover where big ideas come from, how to combine your passions with profits, and how you can reinvent yourself as an innovator
  • Study 4 fascinating case studies from different industries and countries
  • Be inspired and educated by Alexander Blass’s story of how he won the Top Innovator of the Year Award

MASTERCLASS 2 - INNOVATION IN PRODUCT, SERVICE, AND PROCESS DEVELOPMENT

INNOVATION EXPLOSION™: HOW TO IMAGINE AND CREATE BILLION-DOLLAR INNOVATIONS

Description: Particularly in challenging economic times, it is essential to be constantly innovating to stay ahead of the competition by coming up with creative new products and services to market and sell to your current and prospective customers. In this popular content-rich presentation, you will:

  • Grasp the various types of innovation, such as renovation, incremental, revolutionary, breakthrough, and game-changing innovations
  • Learn our proprietary C.R.E.A.T.E.™ business innovation framework containing 6 specific and practical innovation and creativity strategies and methodologies that you can apply to your business immediately to develop big new ideas for innovative products, services, and processes
  • Study 5 international case studies from various industries that have generated billions of dollars in sales, dominated their markets, and beaten their competition using these specific strategies

MASTERCLASS 3 - INNOVATION IN SALES, MARKETING, AND CUSTOMER ENTHUSIASM

HOW TO SELL, MARKET, AND SERVE LIKE AN INNOVATOR: BREAKTHROUGH STRATEGIES TO SKYROCKET SALES AND CUSTOMER ENTHUSIASM

Description: A decisive factor that separates companies with average sales from those with extraordinary sales is their ability to create fanatical, loyal customers and dominate “mindshare”. In this exciting presentation full of breakthrough, state-of-the-art strategies, you will:

  • Study our proprietary T.R.I.B.E.S.™ framework containing 6 key breakthrough strategies and principles for innovation in sales, marketing, and customer enthusiasm
  • Learn how to increase sales and customer loyalty by creating powerful emotional connections between your products and customers
  • Study many international case studies from various industries that have generated billions of dollars in sales, dominated their markets, and beaten their competition using these specific strategies
  • Uncover innovative secrets to generating unparalleled excitement and free worldwide media coverage and publicity for your business
  • Learn how to build innovative “buzz” marketing & viral campaigns, get the world talking, & render your competition obsolete
  • Discover how innovative companies are using psychological principles and state-of-the-art online social media tools to harness the power of crowds and “people power” to market their brands and boost their sales
  • Study innovative customer engagement strategies such as gamification, ethnography, empathy, and crowdsourcing to skyrocket customer service and loyalty

Duration of Masterclasses and Flexibility

  • Each of Alexander Blass’s masterclasses may be conducted over a minimum of 8 hours.  For live events at your location or ours, this is typically done in 1 full day, for example 9am to 5pm.

  • For live-virtual masterclass requests, each masterclass may be conducted either all at once over the course of one full 8-hour day, or presented over multiple sessions (minimum 1 hour each).

  • To complete all 3 innovation masterclasses that cover all the spokes of the Wheel of Innovation™, we recommend you book Mr. Blass for a 3-day masterclass to give your audience an unparalleled learning experience and competitive advantage.

  • Some of our clients “want it all” but have a limited budget, so Alexander also offers a 1-day accelerated masterclass that covers all 3 spokes/modules of the Wheel of Innovation™.  However, for the best experience and learning outcomes for your audience, we highly recommend our standard format masterclasses whenever possible.
